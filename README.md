## Towards Primary Healthcare Improvement in Burundi

This repository contains an evidence-informed policy commentary on how the Burundian government can strengthen Community Health Worker (CHW) programmes by improving motivation, retention, and sustainability.

### Purpose
The paper explores:
- Challenges in financing and retaining CHWs
- Limitations of performance-based financing
- The potential role of community health entrepreneurship
- Ethical considerations and safeguards

### Disclaimer
This is a personal, evidence-informed policy commentary. The views expressed are those of the author and do not represent any institution or government.
